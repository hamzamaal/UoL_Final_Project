Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is our priority. Learn more about how we handle your data.

Introduction

At Food Connector, we are committed to safeguarding the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in accordance with South African privacy laws, including the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Personal details such as your name, email address, and phone number when you register on our platform.
  • Transactional information related to donations, including the resources donated and the recipients involved.
  • Technical data, including your IP address and browser details, to ensure secure and efficient access to our platform.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information is used for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating connections between donors and recipients.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing unauthorized access.
  • Sending updates about Food Connector and related services, if you have opted in.

Your Rights

Under South African law, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or outdated information.
  • Withdraw consent for the processing of your personal information, where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@foodconnector.com.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be communicated through our website. Review the policy periodically to stay informed.
