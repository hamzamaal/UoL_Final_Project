Become a Donor

Why Donate Food?

Food donors play a crucial role in reducing food waste and helping communities in need. By donating surplus food, you not only minimize environmental impact but also support organizations working to feed the hungry.

What You Can Donate

  • Fresh Produce: Fruits and vegetables in good condition.
  • Packaged Foods: Unopened, non-expired packaged items.
  • Prepared Foods: Safe, properly stored meals from restaurants or caterers.
  • Baked Goods: Bread, pastries, and other baked products.
  • Non-Perishables: Canned goods, grains, and dry foods.

How It Works

Donating food is simple and efficient with Food Connector:

  • List the food you wish to donate through our platform.
  • Our matching algorithm connects your donation with local non-profits in need.
  • Coordinate delivery or pickup with the recipient organization.
  • Receive confirmation and see the impact of your donation.

Benefits of Donating

  • Reduce food waste and contribute to a sustainable future.
  • Support local communities and help those in need.
  • Receive tax benefits and recognition for your contributions.
