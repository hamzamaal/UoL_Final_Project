Our Services

What We Offer

At Food Connector, we go beyond connecting donors with recipients. We offer a range of services to ensure the process of donating surplus food is as smooth, efficient, and impactful as possible.

Real-Time Matching

Our platform uses advanced algorithms to connect donors with non-profits in need. By matching donations in real-time, we ensure resources are distributed quickly and effectively.

Donation Tracking

Track your donations from start to finish. Food Connector provides transparency throughout the donation process, offering detailed records of how your contributions are utilized.

Food Safety and Training

Ensure your donations meet food safety standards. We provide training resources and guidelines to help you donate responsibly, aligning with local regulations.

Pickup and Delivery Coordination

Simplify logistics with our coordination services. We help donors and recipients plan pickups and deliveries, ensuring smooth operations and minimal waste.

Community Impact Reporting

Understand the difference your donations make. Food Connector offers comprehensive impact reports, helping you showcase your contributions to stakeholders and communities.
