About Us

Established in 2020 to address widespread food insecurity in South Africa, Lend A Hand connects a world of excess to a world of need by recovering quality edible surplus food from the consumer goods supply chain and distributing it to community organizations that serve the poor. More than 80% of the food received is nutritious. In the 2023/2024 financial year, we distributed 87 million meals and reached 160,000 people daily through a network of 2,500 beneficiary organizations, across South Africa.

Our Vision

A South Africa without hunger.

Our Mission

To reduce hunger by safely and cost-effectively securing quality food and making it accessible to those who need it.

What we do

One third of all food produced in South Africa is wasted, while millions face daily hunger. We partner with farmers, manufacturers, wholesalers, and retailers to recover quality surplus food and redistribute it.

Our Footprint

Our operations span all nine provinces, ensuring we can serve where food insecurity is most prevalent.

Our Beneficiary Organisations

Our network of registered and vetted beneficiaries includes ECD centres, shelters, and community kitchens. 75% of our BOs focus on developmental work with children, women, the elderly, and vulnerable groups.
